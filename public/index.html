Travel

Islamorada, Florida is a very special place to both of us. That is why we decided to have our wedding here, on the beach at the Saxelby home. The Florida Keys are a beautiful part of America that is fairly easy to travel to and within. That said, we wanted to share some tips as most of you will probably be traveling to meet us there.

Flying to Florida

Flying to Miami or Ft Lauderdale are the easiest ways to get to Islamorada from afar. Miami is the closest airport, although Ft Lauderdale is not much further - making either airport possible for your trip. Islamorada is about 90 minutes south of Miami airport and 2 hours south of Ft Lauderdale, meaning that you will need to arrange travel from the airport to your accommodations.

Rental Car
Renting a car is the best option if it is possible. In addition to making your trip from the airport to your accommodations simpler, there are also fun activities, local sights and great adventures in the Keys that are much easier to do if you have a car.

Keys Shuttle
Another great option is Keys Shuttle. This starts at about $80 and they will take you from the airport to your accommodations. However, online reservations may not yet be available as they are still recovering from Hurricane Irma.

Taxi or Car Service
There are many taxi, limo and car services available. Google or TripAdvisor is probably your best bet for this.

Driving to Florida

If you have the time, driving is a great option as you can see all the funny and weird stuff along the roadside, and maybe catch up on some audio books or podcasts. We've both driven many times from NY, it can be very fun. (Just don't stop in Florence, SC... trust us ðŸ˜‚)
